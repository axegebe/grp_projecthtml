Billiards Equipment

What equipment do you need to play billiards?

To play billiards, you will need the following equipment:

  • A billiards table: This is the most essential piece of equipment. Billiards tables come in various sizes, but a standard size is 2.75 metres by 1.4 metres.
  • Billiard balls: You will need a set of billiard balls, which typically includes 16 balls (15 numbered balls and 1 cue ball).
  • A cue stick: This is used to strike the cue ball. Cue sticks come in different lengths and weights, so choose one that feels comfortable for you.
  • Chalk: Chalk is applied to the tip of the cue stick to increase friction and prevent miscues.
  • A triangle rack: This is used to arrange the billiard balls at the beginning of the game.

With these basic pieces of equipment, you can start playing billiards and enjoy the game!
