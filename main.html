Billiards Club

Welcome to the Billiards Club! This is a place for people who are interested in billiards to come together and share their passion. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, you'll find something here for you.

Our mission is to promote billiards as a discipline as a precision oriented and sportsmanship game.
Our vision is to become the primary club for regional players to have a fun and relaxed game to pastime.

The objective of this game isn't really to be competitive. It's just a game where people play to release a bit stress. But in all seriouness, the game of Billiards is about builiding accuracy, focus and boost sharpness in our minds, as every single shot made requires a moment to think and visualize the outcome of the shot.
