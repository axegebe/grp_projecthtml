What is snooker?

Snooker is a cue sport that is played on a large baize-covered table with pockets at each of the four corners and along the sides. The game is played with a set of 22 balls, which consist of a cue ball, 15 red balls, and six object balls (one yellow, one green, one brown, one blue, one pink, and one black).
