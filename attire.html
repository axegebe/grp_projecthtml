Attire

What to wear when you play billiards?

When you play billiards, it is important to wear comfortable clothing that allows for ease of movement. Here are some tips on what to wear:

  • Wear a collared shirt or a polo shirt. This is a common dress code for billiards clubs and tournaments.
  • Choose pants or shorts that are not too tight and allow for flexibility. Avoid wearing jeans or anything that restricts your movement.
  • Wear comfortable shoes with good grip. Avoid wearing sandals or flip-flops, as they can be dangerous on the billiards floor.
  • Avoid wearing hats or anything that can obstruct your vision while playing.

Remember, the most important thing is to feel comfortable and confident while playing billiards. Choose attire that allows you to move freely and enjoy the game!
